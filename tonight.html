Under the Same Sky

A small journey.

Made just for you.

Chapter I

The Sky

Every night, billions of stars shine above us.

Most people look at them for only a moment.

But sometimes...

one star can mean everything.

Chapter II

Distance

No matter where we are...

whether we're sitting beside each other,

or miles apart...

we always share the same sky.

Chapter III

Tonight

Tonight, I'd like to show you something.

Not something fleeting.

Not something made to fade.

Just something that will always be there.

Chapter IV

Leo

Look towards Leo.

Hidden among thousands of stars...

is one that now carries your name.

There it is.

A tiny point of light...

carrying a piece of my heart.
